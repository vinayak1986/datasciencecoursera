Overview

This application estimates & displays Savings Ratio (Aggregate Personal Saving divided by disposable income) using the 4 input values entered by the user : Proportion of population under 15 years old, Proportion of population over 75 years old, Per-capita disposable income & the rate of change of Per-capita disposable income. The estimation is performed using a linear regression model developed from the LifeCycleSavings dataset (Documentation) in R with the Savings Ratio as a function of the above 4 variables.
